Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in your web design packages?

Our comprehensive packages include custom responsive design, premium hosting, SSL certificate, SEO optimization, content management system, contact forms, Google Analytics integration, and 3 months of post-launch support.

How long does the web design process take?

Most projects are completed within 4-6 weeks, following our proven 5-phase process: Discovery, Design, Development, Testing, and Launch. Complex e-commerce sites may require additional time.

We offer comprehensive maintenance plans including regular updates, security monitoring, backup management, performance optimization, and priority support. Our team is available 24/7 for emergency assistance.

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans with 0% interest. Typically, this includes a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining balance split into monthly payments over 3-6 months.

Absolutely! Our content team includes experienced copywriters and SEO specialists who can create engaging, optimized content for your website. We also offer professional photography and video services.
